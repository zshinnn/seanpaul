About

I’m a freelance video and photo editor with a passion for creating content that stands out.

I specialize in editing videos and images, using creativity to bring out the best in every piece of content. I’m also an AI enthusiast, always exploring how smart tools can make the editing process faster and better. Alongside that, I work as a social media booster, helping individuals and small businesses improve their online presence through strategy and engaging visuals.

I believe in clear communication, clean design, and fast delivery. Whether you need a catchy reel, a polished photo, or content that captures attention, I’m here to help.
